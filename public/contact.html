Frequently Asked Questions

We aim to respond to all general inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Technical support requests are typically addressed within 4-8 hours, while application-related questions receive priority response within 12 hours.

To schedule a consultation, please use the contact form above and select "Technical Question" or "Partnership Opportunity" as your subject. Include your preferred dates and times in your message, and our team will coordinate with you to find a suitable slot.

Yes, we welcome in-person visits by appointment. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to schedule your visit. Due to security requirements, all visitors must be pre-registered and bring valid identification.

While our primary communication is in English, we have team members who can provide support in Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. Please mention your language preference in your inquiry, and we'll arrange for appropriate support.
