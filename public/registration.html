Terms and Conditions
1. Program Participation

By participating in the RF Spectrum Sandbox, you agree to operate within the designated parameters and comply with all regulatory requirements set forth by the Nigerian Communications Commission.

2. Testing Guidelines

All testing activities must be conducted within approved frequency bands and power limits. Participants must ensure their activities do not cause harmful interference to existing services.

3. Data and Reporting

Participants are required to provide regular reports on their testing activities and share relevant technical data with program administrators.

4. Intellectual Property

Participants retain ownership of their intellectual property. However, certain technical findings may need to be shared for regulatory assessment purposes.

5. Termination

The program reserves the right to terminate participation for non-compliance with guidelines or if activities pose risks to existing spectrum users.

Privacy Policy
Data Collection

We collect personal and organizational information solely for the purpose of managing your participation in the RF Spectrum Sandbox program.

Data Usage

Your information will be used to evaluate applications, provide program support, and ensure regulatory compliance. We do not share personal data with third parties except as required by law.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@rfspectrumsandbox.gov.ng for any privacy-related requests.
